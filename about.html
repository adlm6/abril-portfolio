About Me

I like computers, music, and taking photos of the world :D

I'm a third-year student at Yale studying Computer Science and Economics. Originally from Mexico, I have a multicultural background that has shaped my perspective on technology, art, and society. I knew I was interested in studying CS ever since I played my first (and favorite) video game: Hollow Knight. Then, a class (along with a really great teacher) in high school introduced me to economics, and the prisoner's dilemma got me hooked.

My coursework has included algorithms, systems, and econometrics, but most of what I know about building came from real experiences: research for UPMC, tutoring in New Haven, internships for a major league baseball team, studying abroad in Shanghai China, and community service in Sado Island, Japan.

Outside of class and work, I capture and edit photography and listen to, compose, and arrange music.
